About Me

UI/UX Designer

I am a passionate Computer Science student with a strong foundation in the field, having graduated from COMSATS University Islamabad. My journey in the world of technology has led me to discover my true passion as a UI/UX designer. Over the years, I have honed my skills and collaborated with a diverse range of clients, gaining valuable experience in crafting user-centered digital experiences. Currently, I am proud to be a part of the dynamic team at Webrange Solutions, where I serve as a User Experience Designer, dedicated to creating intuitive and innovative solutions for our startup's exciting projects. My goal is to continue pushing the boundaries of design, enhancing user interactions, and making a positive impact in the digital landscape.
